Rules & Gameplay


  • General Gameplay

    Normal pickups rules apply
    • Play with a total of 4 players or less on the court play to 15 points.
    • Courts must be scheduled in advance to play with a certified ref
    • Fighting will result in a lifetime ban and an automatic forfeit.
    • Profanity against the ref will result in a temporary ban.
    • Refs and players revied on a scale of 1 to 5 post game on the app.

  • Gameplay Rules

    I. Time Outs
     Each player/team is allowed 1 time out per game. Time Outs are 5 min long to which time expiration clock will stop.

    II. Fouls
    Each player is allowed 5 fouls. A 5th foul will result with the player being terminated from the game.

    III. Points
     3 v 3 games are to 21 win by 2. 1 v 1 and 2 v 2 games are to 15 win by 2.

    IV. Court Size
     Only 3v3 play has the option of full court.

    V. Takebacks
     For half court games. All possesions start behind the 3 point line.

    V. Opening Possession
     1 player will be chosen before the game to shoot for the first possession.

    VI. Time Expiration
    All games are set for 1 hour of play. At the end of 1 hour the game will stop and will result in a final score.

    VII. Shot Clock

    Shot Clock

    24 seconds
